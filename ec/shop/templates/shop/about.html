Ecomm

An e-commerce website is one that allows people to buy and sell physical goods, services, and digital products over the internet rather than at a brick-and-mortar location. Through an e-commerce website, a business can process orders, accept payments, manage shipping and logistics, and provide customer service.

Ecomm is a project developed in Django,HTML/CSS,Bootstrap with database as sqlite3 which focuses on e-commerce. It is one of the projects I've made in my B.Tech. Ecomm was made by Ashwini 0905cs201040 ITM Gwalior. It contains all the major functionalities that a real e-commerce website will have including product view,search,tracker,cart etc. Initially an online marketplace for books, it has expanded into a multitude of product categories.
